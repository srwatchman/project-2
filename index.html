Why Organic?

Conventional cotton uses nearly 25% of the world's pesticides, which hair our air, soil, and water. When used in your babies clothing, it is considerable more concentrated. Since your baby's skin isn't just sensitive, it acutally absorb more of everything it touches.

We are a proud partner with Fair Trade, Ceritifed GOTS factories. These employ local craftswomen from India and offer fair, living wages in addition to clean and humane workspaces.

Our fabrics are also tested rigorously to ensure our dyes and packaging are free from toxins and any other unsafe material. The women we employ know the importance of fabric to a baby’s touch; they’re proud to create bedding and clothing for your children that they would use for their own.
